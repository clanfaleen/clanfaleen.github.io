Homeland History - Valley of The Sands

The Brother's War

Gyryk and Sarok were twin brothers, rivalry between them was always strong from the time of their births. Gyryk was the first of the two to be born, as the first born he would inherit all his parents wealth and belongings the most important of which was the families Manor and Keep. Throughout their lives Sarok held birth order as a slight against him by his brother. Gyryk from birth seemed to be a prodigy at several intellectual studies, while his brother always seemed to be a step behind in his studies. When they were thirteen it was time for advanced studies, the time of apprenticeships. Gyryk was accepted as a student by a well known and highly respected philosopher while Sarok could only find a librarian to accept him as apprentice.

Both their studies went well away from each other, Gyryk began to discover why things in the world worked the way they did, Sarok studied books and manuscripts thought to be lost by the general public. At 16 their father died, leaving Gyryk as the Keep's holder. Gyryk called his brother home shortly after he became Keep Holder and granted half of the lands the family owned to Sarok. Though half was well more than he was entitled too it was not enough for Sarok, in his eye's this granting of property to him was nothing more than Gyryk rubbing his nose in the fact he will never be more than the second child.

Gyryk began to study herbs as a way to heal something that had never been attempted, he had picked a supply of certain herbs he felt had a possibility of having a healing effect. He prepared a sample of an herb and channel some personal energy into it, I believe this personal energy is what you call mana. Though it didn't reveal any healing effects it definitely had an unexpected effect, it is believed this first spell he discovered was a light producing spells. On this day Gyryk became the first mage and took the name Truthseeker. He continued his studies with these, "components" and discovered many more that had effects when channeled into and he also began to combine components to get more amazing results. He divided the components and there effects into 6 categories of magic based on the elements, Earth, Wind, Water, Fire, Light, Dark. He believed that the ability to learn magic was hereditary and attempted to teach it to his brother, but his brother had little interest till he found out about the magic of the element Dark.

Truthseeker cautioned Sarok that the Dark magic should be controlled and not abused and said he would teach none of the Dark magic he had learned to Sarok because he intended to make it so none will even hold it. Sarok again took his brother's actions as a personal slight against him and stole his brother's notes concerning Dark magic and began to study them on his own, he also discovered another set of elements his brother never did based on the element of Death. On the day of this discovery Sarok took the name Deathbringer and vowed to bring death to his brother and any who would support him.

Deathbringer began to amass a large army of undead and demons, he sought others that were willing to serve him and that could learn to use magic. He sent this army against his brother at the families Keep hoping to have his brother killed and have the keep and family property to himself, but his brother and the few students he had, managed to repel the attack and even directed a counterattack. Truthseeker knew what his brother had done when the attack was made on the Keep, so he sent out word to the king asking for his support in eliminating the evil his brother had brought into the world, he also sent out messengers to other countries to seek aid and warn them about the menace.

Truthseeker began to forge a weapon that would be intelligent and able to find a way to defeat his brother without having to kill him. He named the blade "Seeker" and carried it with him to all battles he participated in hoping it would find a way to defeat his brother. The armies of both sides fought many battles but neither truly gained any ground until Deathbringer discovered a spell that allowed for the changing of a persons body into something else. Deathbringer set an ambush for his brother who was leading a small party to a meeting with a new ally.    As soon as Truthseeker came into view Deathbringer sprung his trap casting the new spell at his brother and changing him into a tree, the rest of the party was set upon by various undead as Deathbringer attempted to take "Seeker", a few of Truthseeker's followers attempted to block Deathbringer so he used the spell on himself and changed himself into a large reptile called a velicioraptor, a extremely deadly form coupled with his magic ability he seemed unstoppable, a young mage who came from Clan Snar named, Timeron snatched the blade "Seeker" and fled as the others fought futilely against Deathbringer. He gave the blade one instruction, "Find me those that can defeat Deathbringer anyway possible."

Within 6 months the blade had led him to many people whom all joined him in his cause, they came from varied professions and countries but were united in their cause. They caught Deathbringer by surprise alone at the Keep, during the fight he shifted to the velicioraptor form and managed to kill several of those against him before they managed to kill him. After the battle Timeron hid "Seeker" and used the Keep to set up a school for mages. Those that killed Deathbringer were honored and celebrated, but one by one they each died a mysterious death. Some said these death's were caused by Deathbringer's followers, other's said the evidence pointed to the fact that Deathbringer had somehow returned from the grave.

But that's a story for another time.

This is the story of how it was, remember and learn from it.

The Sisterhood

Fredi was a typical Macote youngster. But she was born with a gift, the gift to manipulate the forces of magic. She discovered her abilities when she was 6, but kept the little tricks she found to herself, until she was 12 when she was caught starting a cook fire without the aid of flint.

The Clan Elders knew the dangers of an untrained person using the magical forces, so they ordered her sent to Askar to train her magical abilities at Timeron's school. Through her training she learned she was strongest in the elements of Fire and Wind and had enough ability to use Earth magic as well.

Her teachers found her to be tough to teach because of her unwillingness to do things exactly the way they showed, she always asked lots of questions as to why it must be done that way when you get a better effect doing another, twice she was almost thrown out of the school for showing a teacher that he or she was wrong when they said it can't be done that way.

In her last 5 years at the school she became friends with the schools guard captain Hylun. When Fredi graduated from the school and was returning home she invited Hylun to go with her, and she accepted.

Eventually the wanderlust got them and they started to travel, taking small mercenary type jobs as the went.

Hylun was born Hyluniaria Sintary Okian to parents that had wished for a boy to carry the name of her father's father, Hylun Sintar, a great Askari general. It is an Askari belief that if you name a child after a departed loved one the child would take on the characteristics of their namesake.

From birth her father talked to her about tactics and strategy, he taught her the strengths and weaknesses of calvary, archers, pikes, footmen etc. At 17 she entered the Askari military for the mandatory one and half year service that all citizens must serve. She was top in her training group and was soon made a junior officer. At the end of her term she signed on for 5 more years. She worked her way up to Captain Commander before her term was up and she left the service to travel.

She went to the territory owned by the Clans as a mercenary, taken on guard assignments and other sword for hire type jobs. She returned to Askar after a few years because the jobs weren't challenging to her. Upon return to Askar her father told her of a mage school looking for a guard captain, this is the same school founded by Timeron after the Brother's war. She took the job and eventually met a young Mage by the name of Fredi a member of the Clan Macote, they became close friends.

When Fredi had learned all she could Hylun returned to the ClanHold Macote with her. Both of them soon felt the need to wander, neither were the type of person that can sit in one spot for a long time. They soon met a beastmaster named Tiana and began the Mercenary group known as the Sisterhood and began to seek others like them.

This is the Story Of Tiana, Beastmaster of the Clan of the Python. Tiana was raised by the Chief Elder of Xaran though she wasn't her child. Tiana was an orphan found on the outskirts of the ClanHold, her true parents are not known but were believed to be hermits. As she grew it became evident that Tiana was special. Beasts from the surrounding wilds would wander into the ClanHold and sit near her, never causing her harm.

At first it alarmed the Clan members but when they realized none of the animals meant her harm they left them be. It wasn't until she was in her early teens that it was realized by the Clan members that she was somehow calling these animals to herself. That realization came when she was mad at a friend and told him that she wished the birds would pull his hair out which they did.

The Chief Elder knew she must find a teacher for Tiana. Beastmasters typically shun civilization, so it wasn’t an easy task but a teacher was found and taught Tiana to control her abilities. Tiana became a young woman, and wanted to use her abilities for the well being of people.

She met a couple of other women , Hylun of Askar, and Fredi of the Clan of the Bear. They had a desire to form a group of female adventurers. The three of them set out to find others who had talent and desire to help others, eventually forming a Mercenary group of all females called the Sisterhood.

In future tellings I will cover other members pasts there were 13 in number, covering all professions and varied races. Tiana was the First, their commanding officer.

This is the story of how it was, remember and learn from it.
