I was born on the small island of Marn. It really is small, not much more than a speck of dirt in the wide blue sea. The original natives, if there were any, had long since fled or been killed by human folk. At some juncture a monastery to Voln was established there...most likely to provide a far away sanctuary for those priestly types and their odd monkish ways. My extended family had landed on this isle about 400 years ago and was 'tolerated' by the locals. They set up a small enclave, worked the land, raised children, crafted beautiful items, and summoned dark spirits and demons...you know, good dark elf stuff.

Actually, the patriarch, my father Thrakus san'Peldar, was a simple man. He had no measurable mystic powers and only small knowledge of the blade. Our family was exiled from our original community for, ummm, shall we say "taking financial advantage of the slow witted". But, as I grew up on the isle, I took to reading the fanciful tales of heroes and wizards in the meager Voln libraries and decided that a warrior and swashbuckler was what I needed to be. Of course the monks had no problems with this. They welcomed me into Voln's embrace simply because the seas around us were high and dangerous....many a ship crashed out in the reefs....and many a dead sailor wandered our beaches.

So I hacked and killed and chopped and maimed and destroyed these vile and powerful undead. I thought myself master of the lands, a hero unparalleled in martial prowess.

But then the fire mages and magess came.

You see, not far away from my small isle, was another larger isle. Adorned with a large and smokey volcano and populated mostly by dwarves. Well, as I said, "mostly populated". Deep in the heart of the volcano lived a rather large community of fire loving mages. With skin of burning sulphur and hair of living fire, they worked their mysteries deep in the heart of Teras Isle. And then, one day, a handful of them conjured up a chariot of ash and flew across the waters to visit a certain Voln monastery.

And killed everyone.

My family, the monks, everyone. All dead. Charred husks and burning bones were all that remained. Oh, and I do mean "everyone". I took my trusty blade in my hand, armed with the conviction of Voln, and marched out to do battle with these "paltry" mages.

And I was killed.

Arcane spells danced about me and dark magics blasted me. My life was snuffed out by spells like a candle in a hurricane. My body was blown out to sea and for many days I floated....my spirit clung to me only due to my many favors of Voln and my conviction to eventually have revenge.

And then, I floated into the landing. A bloated corpse, partially decayed, but not entirely. A cleric whose name is lost in time resurrected me and thus I found myself in the Wehnimer's Landing, with no family, no friends, or most importantly, no silvers or gear.

....well....I'll stop right here for the time being...
