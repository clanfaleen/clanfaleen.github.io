As told by my parents it started on a winter night , they lived in a small hut within a Village of fisher folk along a frozen river just a days walk from Icemule Trace. A young woman knocked on the door of their hut and she carried an infant with her. When they saw her they knew she had not come from any place that they'd ever visited, the material and patterns of her clothing were strange.

She nearly begged them to care for her child, her husband was no where to be seen and when asked she replied that he had remained at their home to defend it with others against an evil so great she would not speak of it except to say that they must never allow her child to seek it out. The woman was of elven heritage and they assumed the child to be elven as well, it wouldn't be until years later they would find him to be actually half elf and half human. The woman disappeared and was never seen or heard from again.

Years passed and I began to notice things that were different between me and my foster parents, first they were much larger then I, they explained that they were known as Giant Folk, their ancestry was a mystery even to them, all they knew was that the Village belong to the Clan of the Icebear. No records were ever written and only passed from Chief elder to Son or heir apparent.

Something was always missing though, I had dreams of a far away place, faces that were hidden in a mist and a great evil that always awoke me in a cold sweat. I got restless and started traveling to Icemule Trace and met many different people there and was befriended by many. A ranger by the name of Freebirrd found me playing around with a lockpick I had found on the ground, it was a strange little devise that I found very interesting. He took me to see a Master Locksmith and I began my training as a Locksmith, less and less did I return to the Village and as a final gift my Father took me aside and showed me the art of fighting, hand to hand, the blade and even introduced me to a Master hunter that used stealth and ambushing techniques.

I spent ten years learning and honing my skills, one day I found another young half-elf maiden by the name of Annilyssa, she reminded me of me when I first entered the city so I began showing her what I knew and introduced her to the Masters who began training her. For many years we hunted, picked boxes and tended to each other and my heart swelled every time I saw her. I asked her to be my wife and she honored me by saying yes. Even with the happiness that I knew, the dreams persisted except strange names began to follow into the dreams one was Vulatarr, his image was of a human dressed in plate armor and a sword almost as tall as he was and he wielded it with the skill of a Master. Confused I asked my parents through a courier about the name, they said the woman had named me after my Fathers brother but she never said my Fathers name so Vulatarr was still a mystery. We moved South to Wehnimers Landing and I could tell Annilyssa was missing something in her life as well, one night as we sat at Thraks Inn tending each other after a long hunt, we heard the thought of Lady Greeka inquiring of youngsters wanting foster parents. I encouraged her to speak with Lady Greeka and soon she was to meet with a possible Father, his name is Lord Sirke. I saw her happiness in being with him as his daughter and my heart swelled for her even more, still the dreams continued only now a female elf was present with the human warrior and he called her Gwenalay. For many years we lived in the Landing and we began to increase our own family by my 25th year we had 7 children and four grandchildren.

Lady Greeka also spoke of a Clan she belonged to, Clan Snar. When I spoke with their leader who was Lord Sirke's brother, their story of their past intrigued me enough to join them. They spoke of a place far away to the North, beyond any known Maps and of a Great Blue Dragon also known as The Blue One who was evil beyond all evil ever known, the names seemed familiar and the evil spoken of as well seemed familiar. They spoke of other Clans within their lands and the Clan named for the Puma seemed right to me or for me. After a couple years I became disenchanted with Clan Snar when a seeker from their home lands came from the wilderness, his Name is Trenmarc of the Clan Faleen when he spoke of bring his own Clan forth in Elanthia I joined him in his cause and became a Tribune. Still the dreams persisted and grew in intensity.

One night I went to Eonakes temple and prayed for guidance and wisdom and an answer to my dreams. To be honest I never expected an answer and definitely not one like this, suddenly he appeared before me in my confusion I leaped back into the bush, my combat instincts kicking in like second thought.

"If you want answers then you must face me and you must be willing to loose everything. Are you willing or shall I leave you to your questions?" he said, his voice seemed to thrum inside of me and it seemed like he yelled but I knew he was barely whispering. Making up my mind I stepped out of the bushes, I knelt before him.

"I have to know what they mean or go mad." I replied.

"To find those answers means you will have to undertake a great quest, greater then any you may have taken before. For this quest you will need a different set of skills and a body that will make the journey possible. Are you ready?" he replied, when I nodded. He waved his hand towards me and I felt a fire burn in me and screamed, I heard popping and cracking sounds and realized they came from inside of my body, I screamed again. I must've passed out at that point because when I awoke I found my clothes were too tight. At a small creek I saw my reflection, I had been transformed into one of the giant folk.

My Family didn't understand, but after my story to them they accepted my new look and condition. Another year passed with me learning new things not only from a Master Warrior but from Trenmarc as well and gaining a new Mother who adopted me during this time her name is Lady Claudeia. Trenmarc spoke of never returning to his home lands because the Blue One had more then likely defeated all opposition, but my heart was set, when the gods told me the time was right I would begin my journey. One night as I slept I dreamed of the strange places once again only this time Eonake was present, "I make no promises that you will find what you seek, I gave you your body to aid you. The lands that you have heard of from Trenmarc or Lajala may or may not be the lands of your history that you will have to find out for yourself. In the morning travel Northwards beyond all you've ever known or heard about, once you are a day past the known lands you will know your way and direction. My word of advise is this, settle your affairs now." he said and I awoke.

With a final farewell I resigned my position from Clan Faleen, told my family good bye and bid farewell to all I had ever known in search for a past that may or may not exist, but then don't we all need to know for sure where we all come from and why we are here?
