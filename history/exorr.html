Lord Exorr Y’ngblood - "The Wolfman"

Exorr’s father was an elf. He lived his life in the wilderness. He spent his time learning about everything around him. He was a master locksmith. You could often find him searching the furthest corners of the forest for chests that pirates and adventurers had left behind.

Exorr’s mother was a wolf. She spent her days stalking through the night looking for food and companionship. She was someone that always kept to herself. All other races looked down upon her because she was not one that walked on two legs like everyone else. Having to deal with rejection all the time she had a very strong will. She enjoyed being with her pack but loved being alone at night and always left the pack when the sun set.

The two of them met one night while Exorr’s father was sleeping on top of a boulder. Exorr’s mother smelled his scent and decided to investigate. That night the forest was bathed in a light that came from the full moon in the sky. This moon was a very special happening that only occurs once every 850 years. Under this moon no being has a form, just their spirit’s can be seen. Neither of them knew about this occurrence. When Exorr's mother found his father that night on the boulder she fell in love with him instantly. Sensing something was different his father awoke to see the most beautiful thing he had ever seen. Both spirits glowed in the moonlight. They inched closer to each other cautiously as neither one of them knew exactly what the other was. The closer they became the greater the love between them grew. The two spirits connected and merged into one, thus Exorr was conceived.

When they awoke the next morning and saw each other for the "first time" neither of them could believe it. They where afraid having always been taught and learned to hate beings of other races. However, remembering the night prior, they both decided they wanted to spend the rest of their lives together no matter how is would look to the rest of there families. Sometimes love is so strong that nothing can keep it apart, this was the case for Exorr’s parents.

Exorr was born 5 months and 3 days after that night. His parents gave him the family name Y’ngblood because they knew if he was given either of they’re two last names he would be hated by all elves and wolves. They decided upon Y’ngblood because Exorr was the first with the name, which gave him the youngest and oldest blood in the family.

Exorr’s mother taught him how to hunt with stealth. She taught him never to kill any wolves or elves and to always help the suffering. Exorr’s father taught him how to become a master locksmith and told him that it would take him a long time to achieve that rank. Both his parents taught him how to hide in the shadows and to stalk people so he could not be detected. They taught him to only steal from those that could spare it and never from the young.

Exorr’s mother passed away when he was only 17 years old. Exorr grieved for months after her death, not wanting to believe she was dead. His father allowed him to grieve; knowing it was the only way that he would be able to accept what had happened. Exorr promised his mother he would always live by her words and never forget her face and the love he felt for her.

Eight years later Exorr’s father passed away from a great illness. Exorr did not know what to do without his mother or father around. He knew that if he went to the wolves they would not accept him because he was half-elf, and he knew that the elves wouldn’t accept him because he was also half-wolf.

Exorr decided that he would leave the forest he knew as his home and search other parts of the world. Before he left his home he promised his mother and father he would never forget their faces, the advice they had given him, and the great love he felt each time he thought about them.

Exorr left his home six months after his father passed away. He explored many places but could never find one that he fancied. One day, while on a search for food, Exorr came across an old warrior. The warrior told him that there was a city just over the mountains to the east, he also told Exorr that once he had explored the city that he should leave the city through the west gate and go northwest until he found a courtyard and that he would know what to do once he got there. Exorr didn’t know if he could believe the old warrior but decided he had to investigate for himself.

The next day Exorr was at the top of the mountains, when he looked down he could hardly believe what he saw. There must have been at least two hundred people bustling about their business, these people where of all different races. Exorr knew he had found his new home and that he would not be looked down upon because of his race.

Exorr made many friends his first year in this city. He had joined a society called Voln that sought after freeing the undead from their torture. Exorr knew his parents would be proud of him for all that he had become.

One day while Exorr was picking some boxes for the locals a little girl looked at him and asked her mother who the wolfman was? Her mother told her that it wasn’t kind to say such things about people. Exorr told the little girl and her mother that he didn’t mind being called the wolfman, he actually liked the sound of it. From that day on Exorr has been known as "The Wolfman" to his closest friends and some of the community.

After a year in the city of Wehnimers Landing Exorr had grown tired of what it was becoming. Many of the new people arriving in the city where rude to Exorr and his friends. Then the word got to them that there was a new city being opened called Solhaven. Exorr and his friends decided to make that city they’re new home. While in Solhaven Exorr met a young girl named Marylee who didn’t seem to know anyone around the city. When Exorr asked her who her parents where she said she didn’t have any and asked him if he would be her father. Exorr was very shocked at this question and didn’t know how to respond. He thought about it and decided that it would be good for him to have a daughter and that this girl needed a parent so he adopted Marylee and has loved her as his daughter ever since.

Exorr has met many great friends in Wehnimers and Solhaven and loves them and their company. He is part of a family, Clan Faleen, that treats him like he’s a brother. Exorr has been in Wehnimers and Solhaven for almost two years but has begun to grow restless. He has begun to think that he will once again set out on a journey to find a new home. However, he will stay in Solhaven until the voices of his parents tell him he must move on....
