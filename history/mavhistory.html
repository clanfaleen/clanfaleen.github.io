Well lets see where to begin I was born in a very remote village far north of Icemule Trace. It was a very uneventful life I was brought up to be a warrior and a hunter. Well one day when I was out training with my elder brother with my little sister watching on as she normally did when my brother and I heard a very unusual noise and he went to investigate. I waited to for him to return I saw for the first time my first troll to my horror I saw my first mountain troll in my rage for its invasion of my home I went to attack it when I heard my little sister scream torn between duty and family I sheathed my sword and grabbed my sister and ran for the village when I got there I saw several of the townsmen had already fallen and I stopped to help drag a fellow clansman to safety setting my sister down and telling her to run for shelter that was the last time I saw my sister. after helping with the others I was told to run for the shelter and told that I must help protect the younger ones with much reservation and a growl of frustration I ran for the shelter as I approached it I heard a growl of battle fury and turned in time to see the most horrible creature I had ever seen later I learned it was a thunder trolls launch a lightning bolt at me. At the last min my older brother jumped in between me and it and I watched my brother die giving his life for me. I was hit by something from behind me and don't remember much after that till I awoke several hours later with my head aching and the taste of blood on my lips. I tried to ask questions but as I looked around I realize I was the eldest member of my clan left alive how could this be we were the strongest clan of northern giantman and we fell to such a lowly creature as a troll. Well to make the story go quicker I spent many days burying the remains of my clans man and friends and family . I spent most of the 10 years after that raising the last of my clans children and trying to figure out what to do when on a hunting trip I came across another hunting party of Giantmen and proceeded to tell them of what had happened and they told me that they would take us all in I accepted for my clansman but I had to leave I had to seek revenge for my clan the trolls have to pay. After a short time to make sure that my clans children were safe I grabbed me some food and weapons and headed south to find where the trolls were so I could slay them all. I shortly came upon a city called Icemule trace where I learned the first things I would learn about fighting monsters. After a short time I decided that I needed to head south I knew the trip would be rough but I had to make it I had to find the trolls. My trip was rough and very short before an ice troll took my head and used my body for bobsled. The only two thoughts in my head while I was waiting to be rezzed was one I am going to kill all trolls and that life had to look up from there.
