On Restday, 1st of Koaratos at 18:00 Elven Stadard time of the year 5101 (Sunday July 1st at 6pm EST 2001) Clan Faleen held a story telling contest. This is the log Edited by Lady Cerridween of the event. This event was part of the Summer Soiree where many houses and a few "unofficial" groups got together and celebrated the coming of summer. The Soiree was kicked off with a parade then over the weekend events were held.

A note from Cerridween: The contest was on the roof of the west tower and lasted about 2 hours from the time I explained rules till I got the stick back. The judges were Lady Talabrie (Herald of our clan at the time) Shirv (Finace officer at the time) Enkai (Fledgling at the time) and myself, Cerridween (Magistrate of the clan) We didn't all say our own stories but rather I started the story and each person had 5 minutes to add to it. It was an idea that came to me to use in GS3 because at sleepover I attended as a child we used to have "pass the stick" scary story nights. It was a blast! It worked in GS3 better than I had ever dreamed. I'd liked to thank everyone who showed up for the event. Without further ado: The Story.
