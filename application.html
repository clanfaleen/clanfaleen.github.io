Application to Clan Faleen

Joining Clan Faleen is not as easy as you may think. We look for bravery, courage, kindness, and giving in all our members. You don't need to be perfect, how many of us really are? ::smiles:: However, we do ask that you respect yourself, other members of the clan, and Elanthia as a whole.

Filling out this application does not make you a member. Myself and the rest of Clan Faleen's officers will review the application and then I will contact you in order to figure out a meeting time that is good for all of us. There are no requirements to join Clan Faleen. We hold no discriminations against anyone's race, class, or religious beliefs.

We hope to meet you soon! ::grins::
