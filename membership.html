Membership

Membership in the Clan Faleen will be open to all inhabitants of Elanthia, regardless of religion, race, color, sex, race, profession or educational background as long as they are dedicated to good role-playing.

All potential members will undergo an Interview with the Herald and at least one Tribune prior to their initiation.

Any player may have more than one character in the Clan as long as they can do the services required for each character.  No player may have more than one character in an Office position.

 Dues

Clan Faleen will require no dues for membership.  Faleen is basically an extended family and as such won't charge to be a member of it.  Each member of Clan Faleen will be required to turn in a minimum of one sack of furs, skins or gems a month the skins will be sold at the furrier and the gems at the gem shop and the proceeds go into the treasury to fund various Clan activities and needs.

These skins or gems can be turned into the Herald, Magistrate or the Master / Mistress of Finances for credit.

If in the event Clan Faleen ever acquires a house any member that wishes to be part of the house must pay the required dues.

Required Services

Each member of Clan Faleen will be required to provide a minimum of 2 hours a month of Community service in order to get the Clan's name known.  These services can be almost anything as long as it is done in the name of Clan Faleen and no personal monetary gain is made.  Below is a partial list of possible services.

These services can be done on ones own and reported to the Master / Mistress of Activities or can be done at a Clan sponsored gather for the purpose of these services.

Lockpicking, Imbedding, Healing, Bashing, Skinning, Foraging, Guiding New Adventurers, Answer new adventurers questions and any other service to the community.

Each member is expect to attempt to make it to 1 of 2 Clan meetings held each month, If you are going to be unable to attend one of the meetings please contact the Herald.
